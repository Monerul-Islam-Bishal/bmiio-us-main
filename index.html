ℹ️ About bmiio.us

bmiio.us is a growing collection of free online tools built for everyday problem-solving. Whether you need to track your health with a BMI calculator, secure your accounts with a password generator, or pick a number with a random number generator, everything on this platform is designed to be fast, private, and completely free.

The idea behind bmiio.us is simple: the internet should have reliable online utilities that anyone can use without signing up, without being tracked, and without paying a cent. Too many websites bury their tools behind paywalls, intrusive ads, or account registration walls. We believe the best problem-solving tools are the ones you can access instantly — open a browser, use the tool, close the tab. That's it.

Every tool on bmiio.us is a no signup tool. Just click, use, and go. All computation happens entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to a server, which means your data stays private. From calculating your body mass index to generating cryptographically secure passwords to creating random numbers for games or raffles, bmiio.us aims to be your go-to destination for free online problem-solving tools.

New tools are added regularly based on user requests and everyday needs. If you can't find what you're looking for, we want to hear about it. bmiio.us is built for you.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are these tools really free?

Yes, 100%. Every tool on bmiio.us is completely free to use with no hidden charges. There are no premium tiers, no paid upgrades, and no "unlock full features" prompts. Our mission is to provide free online problem-solving tools for everyone, and that will never change.

Do you store my data?

No. All tools run entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. When you enter information — like your height and weight in the BMI calculator — it is processed locally on your device and never sent to any server. We do not store, log, or share any data you enter into our tools.

How do you make money?

bmiio.us is monetized through non-intrusive Google AdSense advertisements. We use ads to cover hosting and development costs while keeping the tools themselves completely free. We deliberately avoid pop-ups, auto-playing videos, and aggressive ad formats that ruin the user experience.
